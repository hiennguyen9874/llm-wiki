This wiki is the compiled semantic memory layer of a personal second brain. It is intended to accumulate insights over time by integrating raw sources and crystallized exploratory work into interlinked topic, entity, project, timeline, and synthesis pages.

## What lives here
- Topic and concept pages for durable ideas
- Entity pages for people, organizations, tools, and places
- Project pages for ongoing work
- Source pages for ingested material
- Synthesis pages for comparisons, decisions, and cross-source insights
- Base dashboards in `wiki/bases/` for review, queueing, and operational visibility
- Canvas maps in `wiki/canvases/` for visual synthesis and relationship mapping

## How to use this wiki
- Browse from [[index]]
- Review recent changes in [[log]]
- Start with [[text-to-image-person-retrieval]] and [[synthesis-tbps-hybrid-design-space]] for the current active research cluster
- Ingest new material from `raw/inbox/` or `raw/captures/`
- Save durable analyses to `outputs/` and promote the best ones back into the wiki
- Use Bases for triage/review and Canvases for visual thinking when topics become complex

## Operating principles
- The wiki is persistent and cumulative
- Raw files are immutable
- Exploratory work should often be crystallized before being promoted into durable wiki pages
- Every factual claim cites a source
- Contradictions are preserved and flagged, and stronger evidence can supersede older claims
- Cross-links are part of the knowledge, not optional decoration

## Priority growth areas
- Projects and work
- Research and learning
- Ideas and writing
- People and relationships
- Health, habits, and personal systems

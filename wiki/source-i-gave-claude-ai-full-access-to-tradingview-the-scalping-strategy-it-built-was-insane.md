## What this source is
A YouTube transcript in which the narrator gives Claude Code full access to a TradingView account, asks it to build and optimize an Ethereum scalping strategy, and then walks through the indicators, timeframe choice, and reported backtest results.

## Why it matters
This source extends the TradingView-Claude cluster beyond setup and indicator editing into a more aggressive strategy-generation / optimization demo.

It is useful as durable memory for:
- multi-indicator strategy stacking
- timeframe selection as part of strategy design
- backtest interpretation versus promotional framing
- the difference between a demoed edge and a verified trading edge
